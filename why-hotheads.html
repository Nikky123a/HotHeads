Why HotHeads

Built for the dance floor and the late-night singalong

Bio

Formed in 2005 from the Weekend Warriors competition, Hotheads were initially titled "The Dogs". The success and sheer enjoyment of playing together spurred the band to continue after the first gig and they have been going ever since. Having gained significant experience at over 250 live gigs the boys are now a tight, professional unit that simply love what they do.

We are:

Nikky Crabbe – Lead Vocals
Colin Hardy – Lead Guitar
Nick Garlick – Bass Guitar
Trevor Crump – Percussion
Genre Mix Classic rock, pop hits, indie bangers, and fan favorites.
Live Energy Stage presence that keeps the audience engaged from opener to encore.
Any Venue Perfect for pubs, festivals, private events, and late-night bookings.
